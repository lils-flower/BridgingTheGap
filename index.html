So, what is "</bridging the gap>"?

</bridging the gap> aim to tackle the concerning lack of diversity within the field of CS. We particularly wish to help women, non-binary people, POC and those from economically-disadvantaged backgrounds, though anyone who has an interest in or wishes to learn more about CS can benefit from this site. The purpose is to collate resources related to computer science in one simple place for people to easily access and raise awareness of courses or communities that are specifically trying to help those underrepresented groups.
